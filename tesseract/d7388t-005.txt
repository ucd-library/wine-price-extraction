BORDEAUX

Bordeaux is undoubtedly the most important wine-growing district
in the world. At its best, it is a great wine of balance, richness
and subtlety. Even when it is modest, provided it is authentic,
it is at least a sound pleasing table wine.

Since “Bordeaux” is a geographical term one can approach it
best through a map.

Study the map for several minutes and you will learn more than
by reading pages of boresome description. Such phrases as ““Medoc,”’

‘Sauternes, “Margaux,” “Chateau Margaux” will assume a more
logical, precise meaning.
