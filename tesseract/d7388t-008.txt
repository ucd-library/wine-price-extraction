Burgundy is a wine whose chief characteristics are fullness and
roundness, acquiring softness and bouquet with age.

Observe on the map the extraordinary narrowness and small area
of the Cote d’Or vineyards and you can readily realize why authentic
Burgundy is always scarce and never cheap.
