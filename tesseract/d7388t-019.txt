AMERICAN WHISKIES

The tendency to increase the age of American whiskies without
increasing the price is fast making rye and bourbon even more
popular.

The pre-prohibition whiskies, however, have been soaring in cost
because the supply is small and rapidly diminishing whereas the
demand is constantly increasing. From the viewpoint of good in-
vestment, we consequently advise the purchase of these rare old
whiskies now before they become prohibitive in price, or completely
disappear off the market.

The following list contains a distinguished collection of fine old
whiskies as well as the younger and more popular ones.
